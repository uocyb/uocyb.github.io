Welcome to the UO Cybersecurity Club!


Who are we?

We are a group of UO udergraduates who meet weekly to discuss all things related to cybersecurity!

What do we do?

We invite guest speakers to discuss various topics, enter CTF competitions, volunteer for the annual Cyber Resilience Summit, and more!

When do we meet?

We meet weekly (except for dead week and finals week) on Thursdays at 4:30pm. Check out our calendar page for any changes to the schedule and for information on speakers and topics.

How can I join?

Let us know you're interested! Message us on social media or email us at uocyberinfo@gmail.com and we will invite you to our Discord server where you will find our Zoom meeting links!
